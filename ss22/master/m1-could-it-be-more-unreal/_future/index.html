Many ideas, too little time

Due to very limited time and lack of performance optimization, we weren’t able to include all features the Unreal Engine 5 has to offer. However, what we might to take a closer look on in the future and fully implement it into our showcase demo could be the following:

  • Full utilization of 3D Niagara Fluid Simulation
  • Deeper insight of MetaHuman Creator
  • Extended use of Nanite Virtualized Geometry System

Extension possibilities of Could it be more Unreal?

  • A vast world with more different animals wandering around
  • MetaHuman NPCs you can interact with
  • Online multiplayer with more than 2 players
  • More complex and fun riddles
